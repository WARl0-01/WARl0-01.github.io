I'm not much into pens, but if you're interested, im wirting with a 'Karas Customs' Fountain K (all brass) pen. I'm using a 076 fine point steel 'Block' knib. For ink i'm currently using the 'たけ-すみ' (take-sumi) ink made by pilot, it's a charcoal based ink.

Previously, I was using a random compatibile pen body that fit a Fisher Space Pen refill (pressurized ballpoint.) The refill has a parker style refil adapter and so I just slapped it into whatever pen worked. It's a decent refill, although insanely expensive. I've had issues with it writing poor in the past despite having a lot of ink left, and the fountain pen I use skips wayyyy less, and ink customization and quality is much higher.
